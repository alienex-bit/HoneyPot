# 🍯 HoneyPot

**Capturing Digital Nectar, One Notification at a Time.**

HoneyPot is a premium notification management and digital wellness companion for Android. It intelligently captures your notifications, grades them by priority, and provides beautiful visualizations of your "Digital Hive" activity.

## ✨ Features

- **🍯 Notification Jar**: A history of all your notifications, even those you've cleared.
- **📈 Digital Rhythm**: Beautiful glassmorphism charts (Heatmaps, Radar, Smooth Waves) to visualize your digital stress and routine.
- **🐝 Interactive Insights**: Tap into any notification or grade to see deep stats on your top contributors.
- **🔒 Honey Vault**: Secure your history behind biometric authentication.
- **🚀 One-Tap Launch**: Directly open apps from your history—no more hunting for icons.
- **📱 App Info Hub**: Real-time Play Store descriptions and insights for all your apps.

## 🛠️ Built With

- **Flutter** & **Dart**
- **SQLite** for secure local storage
- **Jsoup** (via `html` package) for real-time app metadata
- **Glassmorphism UI** with standard Material 3 components
